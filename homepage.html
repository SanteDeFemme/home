About Us

Hi and welcome to an introduction of our mission! We are a group of five teenage girls who met through Girls Who Code: Megha, Sophia, Fatima, Alexis and Jwala. Our goal is to empower body image doing this by collecting information in one location. We have created this website as our final project to provide an overall analysis about ones health and to answer any questions one might have.
