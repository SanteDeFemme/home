About Us
Hi and welcome to an introduction of our mission! We are a group of five teenage girls who met through Girls Who Code: Megha, Sophia, Fatima, Alexis and Jwala. Our goal is to empower young women and educate them about the importance of their health. We want to teach them to be confident about their body image and we created this site to provide an overall analysis of one's health.
